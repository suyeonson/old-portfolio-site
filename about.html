My name is Suyeon Son, also known as Summer,

and my passion for impactful journalism carries me from the office of an ex-gang member to random nights of code-a-thons. See a list of my experiences here.


I write about the continuing fight for equal access to education in Chicago, anti-bullying policies that just don't work and other issues of both local and national juvenile justice.

Meanwhile, I look for new ways to develop my fluency with online technologies. In my appreciation of 21st century media as a hub of journalism, engineering and design, I seek to join the ever-increasing community of journo-techies. This website is a product of that curiosity.
